# Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

1. **Download the folder** - You have the complete `nfs-heat-game` folder ready to deploy

2. **Deploy to Netlify (Easiest Method)**
   - Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
   - Drag the `nfs-heat-game` folder into the drop zone
   - Wait for deployment to complete
   - Share your game URL!

3. **Test Locally (Optional)**
   - Use any local web server (Python, Node.js, etc.)
   - Open `index.html` in your browser
   - Start playing!

## First Time Playing

1. Click **SINGLEPLAYER** on the title screen
2. Select your starting car (Street Racer is free)
3. Click the car to start driving
4. Use **W/A/S/D** to drive
5. Press **SPACE** to handbrake for drifting

## How to Earn Rep

- Drive fast (above 80 km/h)
- Evade police (they appear when you go fast)
- Complete cop chases without getting caught

## Buying Cars

- Press **P** during gameplay to open the shop
- You start with $1,000
- Earn more money by driving and staying alive in pursuits
- Purchase better cars to increase your rep earnings

## Tips

- Higher speed = higher risk = higher heat level
- Heat level determines how many cops will chase you
- More cops = more rep rewards (but harder to escape)
- Use tight alleys to evade police
- The Supercar is the fastest but most expensive ($50,000)

## Leaderboard

Click **LEADERBOARDS** on the title screen to see top scores. Your score is saved locally!
